ERA Business Offering

Vision & Mission

Established in 2019, Era is a global independent film studio committed to redefining entertainment through bold, authentic, and culture-defining storytelling. Our vision is to lead a new era of cinema that amplifies diverse voices, embraces cutting-edge technology, and prioritizes sustainability and integrity. We aim to create films that resonate globally while fostering an inclusive and innovative creative ecosystem.

Global Strategy

Era operates as a vertically integrated studio, overseeing development, production, and distribution to maintain creative control and efficiency. We produce 3–5 high-quality films annually, focusing on narratives that challenge conventions and reflect global perspectives. Our global talent network includes award-winning actors, directors, and writers, supported by a robust investment framework with partners in key markets, including a UAE-based investment table.

Sustainability Initiatives

Era is dedicated to eco-conscious filmmaking. We implement carbon-neutral production sets, utilize renewable energy sources where feasible, and prioritize digital distribution to reduce environmental impact. Our goal is to set an industry standard for sustainable practices while maintaining artistic excellence, with a commitment to reducing our carbon footprint by 50% by 2030.

Inclusivity & Diversity

Inclusivity is at the core of Era’s ethos. We ensure 70% of our projects feature underrepresented voices, including women, BIPOC, and LGBTQ+ creators. Our in-house talent roster fosters diversity across all roles, from writers to cinematographers, creating authentic stories that resonate with global audiences. We also partner with cultural organizations to support emerging filmmakers from marginalized communities.

Technological Innovation

Era pioneers the integration of technology in filmmaking. We use AI for script analysis and production optimization, ensuring efficiency without compromising creativity. Our VR initiatives create immersive storytelling experiences, while blockchain technology ensures transparent IP management and royalty distribution, empowering creators and protecting their work.

Revenue Streams

- Theatrical Releases: Targeting $10M–$30M per film in global box office, with 2–3 releases annually.
- Streaming & VOD: Partnerships with major platforms, projecting $15M–$40M yearly.
- International Sales: Licensing to global markets, contributing $8M–$20M annually.
- Merchandising & IP: Expanding IPs into books, games, and merchandise, generating $2M–$10M.
- Grants & Sponsorships: Securing $2M–$5M from cultural and private funding.
Projected revenue grows from $27M in Year 1 to $100M by Year 5.

Investment Opportunities

Era offers equity and profit-sharing opportunities through a global investment network, including a UAE-based table. Our model emphasizes ethical practices, sustainable growth, and high returns, with a projected 15–20% ROI over five years. Investors gain access to a portfolio of innovative films and IP-driven ventures, supported by transparent financial reporting via blockchain.

Strategic Partnerships

Era collaborates with leading streaming platforms, cultural institutions, and technology innovators to amplify our reach and impact. Partnerships with organizations like Sundance Institute and tech firms specializing in AI and VR enhance our ability to deliver groundbreaking content while fostering industry-wide innovation.
