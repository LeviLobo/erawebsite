Vision & Mission

Founded in 2019, ERA is a global independent film studio focused on producing high-quality, commercially successful films that captivate audiences worldwide. Our mission is to deliver innovative, compelling entertainment through creative storytelling and cutting-edge technology, positioning ERA as a leader in the evolving film industry.

Global Strategy

ERA operates as a vertically integrated studio, controlling development, production, and distribution to maximize efficiency and creative output. We produce 3–5 films annually, each designed to appeal to global markets through bold narratives and top-tier talent. Our international network of creators and investors, including a UAE-based investment table, ensures a steady pipeline of projects and capital.

Sustainability Initiatives

ERA adopts cost-effective, future-proof production methods to stay competitive. We use energy-efficient equipment, streamline logistics, and prioritize digital distribution to minimize costs and environmental impact. These practices enhance profitability while aligning with industry trends toward sustainable operations.

Talent Acquisition

ERA collaborates with a diverse range of top-tier actors, directors, and writers to create films that resonate with global audiences. Our talent strategy focuses on securing experienced professionals and emerging creators to deliver fresh, marketable stories that drive commercial success.

Technological Innovation

ERA leverages advanced technology to enhance production and audience engagement. AI optimizes script development and production workflows, reducing costs. VR technology creates immersive viewer experiences, while blockchain ensures secure, transparent management of intellectual property and royalties, protecting profits and creator rights.

Revenue Streams

- Theatrical Releases: Targeting $10M–$30M per film in global box office, with 2–3 releases annually.
- Streaming & VOD: Partnerships with major platforms, projecting $15M–$40M yearly.
- International Sales: Licensing to global markets, contributing $8M–$20M annually.
- Merchandising & IP: Expanding IPs into books, games, and merchandise, generating $2M–$10M.
- Grants & Sponsorships: Securing $2M–$5M from private and industry funding.
Projected revenue grows from $27M in Year 1 to $100M by Year 5.

Investment Opportunities

ERA’s exceptional talent, including award-winning actors, directors, and writers, unlocks access to substantial financing from our global investment network. Their proven track record and ability to deliver compelling, marketable films attract high-value investors, ensuring robust funding for premium cinematic projects. ERA offers attractive investment options through equity and profit-sharing, supported by a global network including a UAE-based table. With a projected 15–20% ROI over five years, investors benefit from a diversified portfolio of films and IP ventures, backed by transparent financial tracking via blockchain technology.

Strategic Partnerships

ERA partners with leading streaming platforms and technology firms to expand market reach and integrate innovative tools. Collaborations with industry leaders ensure our films achieve maximum visibility and profitability, while tech partnerships drive advancements in production and distribution.
