Meet Our Team

Levi Lobo

Founder and CEO of ERA, Levi Lobo is an actor and producer from Albuquerque, New Mexico, with Mestizo heritage. Known for roles in Fast Color, SGT. Will Gardner, and The Weight of It All, Levi brings raw authenticity to his performances. His producing work, including Instructions for Yesterday and Consider the Violence of Cutting Roses, reflects his commitment to bold, diverse narratives. Levi’s vision drives ERA’s mission to craft stories that resonate deeply.

Dannie McCallum

Dannie McCallum, a rising star with Mexican heritage, shines in Echo and Here. Her grounded yet powerful performances reflect her deep connection to community and culture. As a key collaborator at ERA, Dannie brings empathy and resilience to her roles, contributing to projects that challenge conventions and inspire audiences.

Ollie Roitt

Ollie Roitt brings over 13 years of expertise in public relations and Family Office management to ERA, with a track record of delivering results for C-suite executives in financial services and corporates. Having worked with leading firms like CEB and Reed Business Information, Ollie enhances ERA’s global outreach and investor relations, ensuring strategic partnerships and financial growth align with the studio’s creative vision.

Maen Samara

Maen Samara, a visionary entrepreneur, leads ERA’s strategic production efforts. Based in Dubai since 2011, Maen founded Virtua, a scalable HR and payroll solution, later acquired by the Al Serkal group and rebranded as Trans Skills, serving over 40 countries. His leadership, marked by precision and innovation, drives ERA’s global production strategy, ensuring bold, impactful cinematic projects.
